6 Quick Snacks for Busy Days

By Bharathi T
Published on September 18, 2024

If you're always on the go but still need a quick snack fix, these six recipes are for you! Each snack can be whipped up in minutes, ensuring you stay fueled throughout the day. Let's dive into these quick and tasty treats.

This crispy, savory snack is a favorite across India. Stuffed with spiced potatoes and peas, samosas are easy to make and perfect for a quick snack or side dish.

Pasta is an all-time favorite snack. Layered with cheese and toppings, it's quick to assemble and always a hit!

Mini Pizzas

Bite-sized pizzas using store-bought dough, topped with your favorites, and baked in minutes for a tasty snack.

Fruit & Nut Energy Bars

Made with dates, almonds, and oats, these bars give you a quick energy boost while satisfying your sweet cravings.
